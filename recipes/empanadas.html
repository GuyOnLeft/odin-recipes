Real Empanadas from Argentina

Description

Let's have a little chat about these. In November of 2021, I visited Buenos Aires and was baffled about the food scene. I had no idea that:

  1. There was a thriving food scene here.
  2. That the food has a heavy european influence. Primarily, Italian and Spanish.
Empanadas were by far the most popular dish available throughout the country and I'm going to show you today the one's that my now girlfriend has taught me.

Instructions
